Saeed Dupree



Hey everyone, I am Saeed Dupree, the senior researcher in the M-PURE program. I am a Junior at JCSU, majoring in Chemistry and Biology. At JCSU, I am a part of the Track and Field team and the Pre-Med Club, and I enjoy doing 3D printing as a hobby. After JCSU, I plan to attend medical school and become a urologist. In the M-Pure project, I am the person in charge of running experiments on the Cellulose NF in the FTIR, TGA, and DSC. The M-Pure program will help me better understand polymers and the structural changes that happen when heat is applied, which will help me better understand polymers and their medical applications during medical school. hashtag #mpureresearch
